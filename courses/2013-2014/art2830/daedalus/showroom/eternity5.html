A Space Odyssey

If you dreamed of being an Astronaut as a child but never made the grade to go to space we have the perfect adventure for you. In your Space Odyssey you will get to pick your ship. Are you a Miner, searching for precious metals on asteroids? Are you an intrepid explorer, travelling to the farthest reaches of the galaxy? Are you a colonizer, looking for a planet to call home? No matter what your choice, you will also get to pick a crew of 8 from your circle of friends, but choose wisely because space does funny things to a man.
