A Builder's Dream

Builders dream of making things. Big things, small things, intricate, simple, plain, ornate. They just want to build. With this package, that is exactly what you will be doing only on a much grander scale. A Builder's Dream allows you to create the Solar System as you see fit. Watch as your planets change. See the Earth emerge and watch as evolution begins. Will you save the dinosaurs from extinction to see what it would be like for man to have to compete with such fierce hunters? Which comes first, the chicken or the egg? You will finally know! With the Builders package you get to decide!

Basic package includes:

2 full cycles of the solar system from birth to destruction. Use them wisely!
Unlimited Natural Disasters
A choice of lifeforms and their evolution
