Enjoy the Solitude

Are you a Lone Wolf? Many people identify themselves as introverts and would prefer to be alone than amongst others. If you count yourself among the people who find it taxing to interact socially with others then Enjoy the Solitude. With this experience you will find yourself alone for the majority of your time. At random intervals you will have serendipitous encounters with easy going strangers and you will be allowed to schedule visitors in your day planner, which we will virtually provide, once a month for up to 3 days at a time. The rest of your time is just that, yours! Obligation free, you can choose to catch up on the reading you had been meaning to do. You can watch movies, lounge by the pool, slowly sip your coffee while watching the sun rise, all with the pleasure of knowing that no one will interrupt you beautiful solitude.

Upgrades include:

Pleasant wait-staff at restaurants
The ability to choose a Partner who does or does not live with you
A Weather control device
More scheduled visits and serendipitous encounters
